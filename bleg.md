We consider the random function \\( H_N(\sigma) = \sum_{i,j=1}^{N} g_{ij} \sigma_i \sigma_j \\) where the \\( g_{ij} \\) are i.i.d standard Gaussian and \\( \sigma \in \\{ \pm 1\\}^N \\).

\begin{equation}
H_N(\sigma) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{N}} \sum_{i,j=1}^{N} g_{ij} \sigma_i \sigma_j \, ,
\end{equation}
